Hi there! We’re glad that you are taking a peek at our job board. Here at Axilweb, we are a passionate, fun-loving, growing team. We are looking for a Front-end Engineer to join our team and grow with us.

We take on challenging projects, work creatively, and create new customer experiences.

  • Collaborate with UX/UI designers to create visually appealing and intuitive web pages
  • Translate Figma files into working HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript code
  • Collaborate with back-end developers to integrate front-end code with back-end APIs and services
  • Work with the marketing team to develop and maintain landing pages and email templates
  • Maintain and optimize front-end code libraries for reusability and scalability
  • Ensure responsiveness and cross-browser compatibility
  • Research and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in front-end development
  • Passion and positive attitude with a willingness to learn new technologies
  • Be open to receiving feedback and constructive criticism
  • Use project management tools like Basecamp to track and manage project tasks and deadlines
  • Provide daily updates to team leaders and project/product managers
  • Take ownership of tasks and projects and ensure timely completion with high-quality results
  • Experience with JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SASS/SCSS, Tailwind, Bootstrap 5
  • Hands-on experience with JS Frameworks such as VueJS or React
  • State management libraries like - Vuex/Pinia/Redux
  • Knowledge of SEO best practices and optimization techniques
  • Experience with Git/GitHub for version control
  • Ability to understand and meet W3C standards and Google PageSpeed Insights
  • A track record of writing clean, high-quality, high-performing, scalable code
  • Working knowledge on Gulp
  • Understanding of PHP and Laravel/WordPress
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Design, develop, and implement custom Shopify themes and modify them using Liquid
  • Develop Shopify custom app and theme app extension from scratch.
  • Strong knowledge of the Shopify platform
  • Familiarity with the Shopify API and GraphQL for interacting with store data
